Problem Statement.
Let's see if the examples can give us some inspiration. Example 0 is pretty obvious and Example 1 is pretty clear from their picture. Let's look at example 2 drawn on graph paper:

See the pattern?

  • The rectangle's left side will be on the minimum X value.
  • The rectangle's right side will be on the maximum X value.
  • The rectangle's bottom side will be on the minimum Y value.
  • The rectangle's top side will be on the maximum Y value.
So, you need to calculate those minimum and maximum values and use them to calculate the area.
