Scores

This was a challenging exam, no doubt because of the high number of multiple choice questions. Unfortunately for y'all, that's an artifact of having such high enrollments.

Here's the mapping of test scores to grades:

  • 69 - 77: A+
  • 60 - 69: A
  • 48 - 60: B
  • 38 - 48: C
  • 30 - 38: D
  • Below 30: F
