Comments

This is probably the easiest exam I've given in nearly 20 years of teaching. That said, it did a pretty good job of testing you. Questions 11 and 12 had a pretty big impact on your score, but that was intentional, since there was not a huge amount of code reading/writing on the exam.

The ranges of grades are below. I am not breaking up grades into "B+" or "A-". Instead, I simply break them up into ABCD, and use those scores to calculate your final scores. When I get the final scores, I will break up the B's into A-/B+/B, and the C's into B-/C+/C.

  • A+: 40 to 46
  • A: 35 to 40
  • B: 30 to 35
  • C: 24 to 30
  • D: 20 to 24
  • F: Below 20
